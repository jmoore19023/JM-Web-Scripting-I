About Me

Expanding my skillset and looking for ways to apply it to real problems, whether that leads into IT, data analysis, or something in between.

See My Projects

I've spent over 8 years at GPI working my way up from operator to Continuous Improvement Specialist. Every step taught me something new about how people, processes, and data connect on the plant floor.

I originally started my Associate's in Applied Science for Programming about 10 years ago but stepped away to provide for my family. I'm back now to finish what I started, with a much clearer picture of where I want to go.

The turning point came when I facilitated an MES rollout at our facility. Watching what was possible when that data was leveraged correctly flipped a switch. Since then I've been focused on automating manual reporting and connecting our QMS, MES, and ERP systems to eliminate wasted manhours.
