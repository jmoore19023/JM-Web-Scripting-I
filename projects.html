Ignition MES Implementation

Role: CI Facilitator  |  Scope: 100+ Machines  |  Platform: Inductive Automation Ignition

▣ The Problem

Our facility lacked real-time visibility into machine performance across over 100 pieces of equipment, including presses, extruders, formers, cutters, and thermoformers. Operators had no live KPI feedback, and data collection was inconsistent and manual.

⚙ My Role

I facilitated the full MES rollout at our facility, coordinating across department managers, the plant electrical engineer, and corporate IoT teams. Key responsibilities included:

  • Keeping the rollout on schedule and escalating issues as they arose
  • Verifying tag values and system outputs as equipment was brought online
  • Coordinating between on-site teams and corporate IoT to ensure solutions were properly validated
  • Identifying and organizing locations for KPI displays and dashboards as visual aids for operators
  • Training managers and operators on data entry and platform usage as super users

∑ The Outcome

Operators now have live visibility into their performance through strategically placed KPI dashboards. Data that previously required manual collection is now captured automatically through the MES, laying the groundwork for deeper cross-system analysis.

Cross-System Reporting & Data Integration

📊

With the MES now in place alongside our existing QMS and ERP systems, the real opportunity became connecting them. By merging data across all three platforms, leveraging ODBC connections and API responses to pull data directly from source systems, I'm able to surface insights for the plant that simply weren't visible when looking at any one system in isolation. This eliminates manual exports and ensures reports always reflect live data, giving leadership a fuller picture of performance, quality, and operational efficiency in one place.

Using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, and BI tools, I built and maintain reports that automate data pulls from these systems, eliminate manual entry, and deliver consistent outputs that drive better decisions on the floor.

Tools: Microsoft Excel · Power Query · BI Tools · Access Databases · MES · QMS · ERP
